Alessandro Marin Vargas

I am currently a PhD student in the A. Mathis group at the EPFL. My main goal is to reverse engineer the computational mechanisms that govern the sensorimotor system to improve the control of robots and neuroprostheses. My main interests lie at the intersection between Artificial intelligence and Neuroscience.
